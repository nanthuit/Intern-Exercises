KERALA BOAT HOUSE

A Kerala boathouse is a traditional houseboat that offers a peaceful journey through the scenic backwaters of Kerala. It is surrounded by lush greenery, coconut trees, and beautiful waterways. Visitors can enjoy authentic Kerala cuisine, stunning sunsets, and the calm beauty of nature. A boathouse stay provides a unique and relaxing experience, making it one of Kerala's most popular tourist attractions.

RED FORT

The Red Fort is a historic monument located in Delhi and was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in 1648. It is famous for its magnificent red sandstone architecture and rich cultural heritage. The fort is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site and attracts millions of visitors every year. It stands as a symbol of India's history, freedom, and architectural excellence.

LOTUS TEMPLE

The Lotus Temple is a beautiful place of worship located in Delhi, India. It is famous for its unique lotus-shaped architecture made of white marble. People of all religions are welcome to visit and pray in its peaceful atmosphere. The temple is one of India's most popular tourist attractions and symbolizes unity, peace, and harmony.
